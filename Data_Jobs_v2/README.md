# Data Jobs Dashboard v2 w/ Power BI

![Dashboard Page 1](/images/V2.gif)

## Introduction

Navigating the data job market can feel like a maze with information scattered everywhere. This dashboard (V2.0) is designed specificakky for **Job Seekers, Job Transitioners, and Job Swappers** to cut through the clutter! Using a real-world dataset of 2024 data science job postings-rich with details on titles, salaries, and locations-this project offers a streamlined, single-page interface to quickly explore crucial market trends and compensation insights.

## Skills Showcased

- **Dashboard Design:** Crafting an intuitive and visually appealing report layout.
- **Power Query ETL:** Performing data cleaning, shaping, and transformation.
- **Data Modeling:** Building eefficient data models with relationships (Star Scheme principles).
- **DAX Fundamentals:** Creating calculations and aggregations to derive key insight.
- **Vizualizations Utilized:**
    - **Core Charts:** Column, Bar, Line, and Area charts for comparisons and trends.
    - **Map Charts:** For displaying geospatial data.
    - **Cards:** To highlight key performance.
    - **Tables:** For presenting detailed, tabular information.
    - **Chart Variety:** Selecting from common and uncommon charts types for effective storytelling.
- **Interactive Features:**
    - **Slicers:** Enabling dynamic, user-driven data filtering.
    - **Buttons & Bookmarks:** For streamlined navigation and managing report views (including Drill-Through).


## Dashboard Overview

The second iteration consolidates the dashboard into a **single, focesed page**, designed to give job seekers the most critical insights at their fingertips.

![Dashboard Page 1](/images/v2_page.png)

This page acts as your concise mission control for the data job market. It showcases key performance indicators (KPIs) like **JOb Count, Skills Per Job, Median Yearly Salary, Median Hourly Salary**. You can also quickly see **Skill Popularity** (by job percent or count) and compare **Salaries different Job Titles**, all designed for an efficient overview.


## Conclusion

This updated dashboard (Version 2.0) showcases Power BI's ability to transform extensive job posting data into a powerfull, streamlined tool for career analysis. It empowers **Job Seekers, Job Transitioners, and Job Swappers** by allowing them to filter and explore essential market insights efficiently on a single page, helping them make informed decisions about their next career move.
